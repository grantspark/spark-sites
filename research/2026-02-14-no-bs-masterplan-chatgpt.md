# The No Bullshit Marketing Masterplan for SmBz — ChatGPT Project Import

## Context

HotSpark = merged company (Influencer Marketing + Website Design/Support). Marketing Training and Services company. Helps small business owners and entrepreneurs starting out or scaling from mid-6 to upper-6/7 figures. Trains on marketing, leads, marketing systems.

**Authors:** Grant Sparks, Nicole Bradham (possibly Jon Sierra on Brand/Design, Amber Beardmore)

**Format:** Workbook, 9th grade reading level, relatable

**Structure:**
- Part 1: Setup (Laying the Foundation) — Chapters 1-30
- Part 2: Starting (Getting Leads and Making Sales) — Chapters 31-60
- Part 3: Sales (Turning Conversations Into Conversions) — Chapters 61-90
- Bonus Part 4: Speeding Up with A.I. — Chapters 91-119

## Quick Start Guide (Derivative)

A carved-out 7-day version of the masterplan covering:
- Day 1: Canva brand guide, domain purchase
- Day 2: Google Workspace email, Google Business listing
- Day 3: Google Voice, appointment booking
- Day 4-5: Website launch (Flywheel + WordPress)
- Day 6: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn profiles
- Day 7: First Facebook ad

Positioned as free lead magnet option. Written by Grant + Nicole.

## Pain Points Identified (from ChatGPT exploration)

### Lead Generation
1. Lack of clear target audience
2. Ineffective marketing strategies
3. Limited budget
4. Inconsistent efforts
5. Overreliance on referrals

### Prospecting
6. Finding qualified leads
7. No systematic approach
8. Time constraints
9. Low response rates
10. Can't reach decision-makers

### Sales Pipeline
11. Weak follow-up
12. No pipeline visibility
13. Inconsistent sales processes
14. Long sales cycles
15. Difficulty closing

### Marketing & Outreach
16. Content creation overwhelm
17. Inadequate online presence
18. Can't leverage social media
19. No email strategy
20. Low brand awareness
21. (Bonus) Technology overwhelm
