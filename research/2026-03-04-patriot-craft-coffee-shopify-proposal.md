# Patriot Craft Coffee — Shopify Migration Proposal

Prepared: March 4, 2026
Client: Patriot Craft Coffee — Chris MacArthur
Project: WordPress to Shopify Full Migration
Prepared by: Spark Growth Marketing

---

## Overview

Full migration from WordPress/WooCommerce to Shopify. Scope includes design,
product migration, subscription setup, wholesale/B2B portal, USPS live shipping
rates, and launch. Work delegated to E2M. Spark manages project, client comms,
QA, and launch.

---

## Scope and Time Estimates

1. Discovery and Architecture Planning — 4–6 hrs
   Review WP site, map products, page structure, customer segments, and shipping
   zones. Define theme, app stack, migration approach. Deliverable: signed-off
   project brief before build.

2. WordPress to Shopify Site Migration — 20–30 hrs
   - All products (descriptions, images, variants, pricing)
   - Blog posts and content pages
   - Customer accounts (via Shopify import tools)
   - Navigation and footer
   - SEO 301 redirects (old WP URLs → new Shopify URLs)
   - Theme setup and design customization
   Assumes 30–80 products, 5–15 pages.

3. Subscription App Setup — 8–12 hrs
   - Shopify Subscriptions or Recharge (TBD in discovery)
   - Product tagging, frequency options, customer portal
   - Email notifications, dunning/payment failure handling
   - Full test order cycle
   App fees (Recharge etc.) are client-paid.

4. Wholesale / B2B Restricted Product Page — 8–12 hrs
   - Gated wholesale catalog (password or account-based)
   - Wholesale pricing separate from retail
   - B2B account creation workflow
   Uses third-party app (Wholesale Club, Locksmith) on standard Shopify plan.
   App costs are client-paid. Shopify Plus ($2,300/mo) is an option if volume
   justifies it — will recommend in discovery.

5. USPS Live Shipping Rate Integration — 6–8 hrs
   (Relates to open ClickUp task "Patriot - Chris - USPS API")
   - USPS carrier-calculated rates via Shopify Shipping
   - Package dimensions and weights configured per product
   - Shipping zones and optional flat-rate/free shipping thresholds
   - Test rate display at checkout
   Requires Shopify plan ($79/mo) or higher for third-party calculated rates.

6. QA, Testing, and Launch — 8–10 hrs
   - Cross-browser and mobile testing
   - All checkout flows (retail, subscription, wholesale)
   - Shipping rate verification
   - Test payment orders
   - 301 redirect audit
   - DNS cutover and domain connection
   - 48-hour post-launch monitoring
