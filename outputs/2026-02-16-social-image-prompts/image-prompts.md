## Usage Notes

- **Vertical crop safety:** All subjects are described as centered in the middle third. When cropping to 1080x1350 (Instagram/Stories), the left and right edges can be trimmed without losing key characters.
- **Consistency:** Every prompt includes the same character descriptions, space suit details for Ember/Nova, and solarpunk + Ghibli style anchors.
- **Brand colors:** Coral-to-pink gradient (#FF6B47 to #E91E8C) referenced as accent lighting/glows throughout.
- **Generation tool:** These prompts are optimized for ChatGPT/DALL-E, Midjourney, or similar. For Midjourney, append `--ar 1200:630 --style raw` to each.
