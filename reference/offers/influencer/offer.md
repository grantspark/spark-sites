# Influencer Marketing

## What It Is

Influencer networking events and soft openings. Connecting local businesses with content creators to generate buzz.

## Who Leads This

**Nicole Rhea** — Manages all influencer relationships and event coordination.

## Pricing

Custom per event. Factors:
- Venue/business type
- Number of influencers
- Event scope
- Content deliverables

## What's Included

- Influencer recruitment and vetting
- Event coordination
- Content capture guidance
- Post-event reporting

## Use Cases

- **Soft openings** — Generate buzz before official launch
- **Grand openings** — Maximum visibility for new locations
- **Product launches** — Get products in front of content creators
- **Seasonal campaigns** — Holiday events, anniversary celebrations

## How It Works

1. Client books influencer event
2. Nicole coordinates with local influencers
3. Influencers attend, create content
4. Content gets posted, business gets exposure
5. Often leads to ongoing marketing relationship

## The Upsell Path

Influencer Event → Social Media Management
Influencer Event → Facebook Ads (boost influencer content)
Influencer Event → Ongoing marketing services

## Best For

- Restaurants and hospitality
- Retail stores
- Local service businesses with physical locations
- Event-driven businesses
